## Part 1: Getting Started

### 1.1 What is Game Shelf? âœ…

Game Shelf is a free app that tracks all your daily puzzle games in one place.

**The Problem It Solves:**
- You play multiple daily puzzles (Wordle, Connections, Strands, etc.)
- Each game tracks stats separately
- Hard to see your overall progress
- Easy to forget which games you've played
- Streaks get lost when you forget a day

**What Game Shelf Does:**
- Tracks 36+ daily puzzle games in one dashboard
- Maintains streaks across all games
- Shows unified stats and progress
- Lets you compete with friends in "Brain Battles"
- Provides AI hints when you're stuck
- Makes sharing results easy

**Key Features:**
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Unified Tracking** | All games, one app |
| **Streak Protection** | Never forget which games you've played |
| **Stats Dashboard** | See your performance across all puzzles |
| **Brain Battles** | Compete with friends over multiple days |
| **AI Hints** | Get help when stuck (5 tokens each) |
| **Social Features** | Friends, leaderboards, nudges |
| **Share Hub** | Easily share your results |

**Free vs Premium:**
- **Free:** All core features - tracking, stats, streaks, friends, battles
- **Tokens:** Free currency earned through play (for hints)
- **Coins:** Optional purchase for premium rewards and higher-stakes battles
- You never need to pay to use Game Shelf

4. Game Shelf detects the result and shows "Tap to Log" card

**iOS Permission Prompt:**
When you see "Allow Paste?" on iPhone:
- Tap **Allow** to let Game Shelf read your clipboard
- This is a privacy feature - no app can secretly read your clipboard on iOS

- Clipboard is empty (you didn't copy the share text)

- Share text was modified (extra text added)
- Clipboard contains something else (copied after sharing)

**Troubleshooting:**
1. Go back to the game and tap Share again
2. Return to Game Shelf immediately (don't copy anything else)
3. If "Tap to Log" still doesn't appear, use the Record Game button manually
4. As a last resort, long-press Record Game for manual entry

## Part 6: Streaks

### 6.1 How Streaks Work âœ…

Streaks track consecutive days of play for each game.

**What is a Streak?**
- A streak counts how many days in a row you've played a specific game
- Each game has its own independent streak
- Playing today extends your streak; missing a day resets it to zero

**Streak Example:**

| Day | Action | Streak |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Monday | Play Wordle | 1 |
| Tuesday | Play Wordle | 2 |
| Wednesday | Play Wordle | 3 |
| Thursday | **Miss** | **0** (reset) |
| Friday | Play Wordle | 1 (starts over) |

**Key Streak Rules:**

1. **One play per day counts:** Playing twice on Tuesday doesn't count for Wednesday
2. **Calendar day based:** Not 24-hour windows, but actual dates
3. **Each game is separate:** Missing Wordle doesn't affect your Connections streak
4. **Max streak remembered:** Your best streak is saved even after resets

### 6.2 Streak Timing âœ…

Understanding when streaks reset is crucial for maintaining them.

**The Basic Rule:**
- Streaks reset at **midnight in your local timezone**
- You have until 11:59 PM to record that day's game
- At 12:00 AM, a new day begins

**Your Timezone vs. Game Timezone:**

Many games reset at different times than midnight:
- **Wordle:** Resets at midnight Eastern Time
- **Connections:** Resets at midnight Eastern Time
- **NYT Mini:** Resets at 10 PM ET (day before)

**But Game Shelf tracks YOUR play:**
- What matters is when YOU record the result
- If you play at 11 PM your time, it counts for that day
- Game Shelf uses your device's timezone

**Common Scenarios:**

| Scenario | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Play Wordle at 11:30 PM Monday | Counts for Monday |
| Record at 12:05 AM Tuesday | Counts for Tuesday |
| Play at 11 PM, record at 12:30 AM | Counts for Tuesday (record time matters) |

**Edge Case: Late Night Play**

If you're playing near midnight:
1. Finish the puzzle
2. Copy the share text immediately
3. Record in Game Shelf **before** midnight
4. If you miss midnight, it counts for the next day

**Travel and Time Zones:**

- Game Shelf uses your device's current timezone
- If you travel: your phone clock adjusts, so does Game Shelf
- International travel may cause apparent streak gaps
- Best practice: Record before traveling across time zones

**"New Puzzle Available" vs "Streak Day":**

| Event | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| New puzzle available | Game server released today's puzzle |
| Streak day in GS | Calendar day you recorded a result |

These can be different! A puzzle released at 10 PM ET appears as "today's" puzzle but recording it counts for whatever YOUR calendar day is.
