# Game Shelf Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 30, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how David Stewart ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Game Shelf ("the Service"), including our website at gameshelf.co and companion games (Quotle, Slate, Rungs, Word Boxing).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**  
When you sign in with Google, we receive:
- Your name
- Email address
- Profile photo URL

**Game Data**  
When you use the Service, we collect:
- Puzzle game results you record
- Statistics and streaks
- Battle participation and results
- Friend connections
- Display name you choose

**Transaction Information**  
When you make purchases, we collect:
- Purchase history (items, amounts, dates)
- Payment confirmation from Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)

**Communications**  
When you contact us, we collect:
- Feedback and support requests
- Bug reports
- Suggested games

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**  
We automatically collect:
- Features you use
- Pages you visit
- Actions you take (games logged, hints requested)
- Time spent in the app

**Device Information**  
We collect:
- Device type and operating system
- Browser type
- IP address
- General location (city/region level, not precise)

**Cookies and Similar Technologies**  
We use local storage and session storage to:
- Keep you signed in
- Remember your preferences
- Cache game data for offline use

We use your information to:

**Provide the Service**
- Track your game results and statistics

**Process Transactions**
- Complete purchases

- Develop new features
- Optimize performance

**Communicate with You**
- Respond to support requests

**Ensure Security**
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

**With Your Consent**  
We share information when you direct us to, such as connecting with friends.

**With Friends**  
Friends you connect with can see:
- Your display name and photo
- Your leaderboard rankings
- Your activity status (games played today)
- Your friend code

Friends cannot see your full game history, detailed statistics, or private information.

**Service Providers**  
We use trusted third parties to help operate our Service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Google Firebase | Database, authentication, hosting | Account data, game data |
| Stripe | Payment processing | Purchase transactions |
| Anthropic | AI hints | Hint requests (anonymized) |

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

**Legal Requirements**  
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

**Business Transfers**  
If we merge with or are acquired by another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

---

## Data Retention

**Active Accounts**  
We retain your information while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service.

**Deleted Accounts**  
When you delete your account:
- Your personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days
- Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics
- Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before deletion

**Transaction Records**  
We retain purchase records for 7 years for tax and legal compliance.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

**Right to Know**  
You can request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and disclose.

**Right to Delete**  
You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

**Right to Non-Discrimination**  
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

**Do Not Sell**  
We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

To exercise these rights, contact support@gameshelf.co with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:

**Legal Basis for Processing**  
We process your data based on:
- **Contract:** To provide the Service you requested
- **Legitimate Interest:** To improve and secure the Service
- **Consent:** For optional features like AI hints

**Your Rights**  
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

**Data Transfers**  
Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We use Firebase and other providers that maintain appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

To exercise these rights, contact support@gameshelf.co with "GDPR Request" in the subject line.
