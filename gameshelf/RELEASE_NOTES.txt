COMPREHENSIVE BATTLE SCORING REBALANCE
--------------------------------------
Philosophy: Above-average play wins battles, rare achievements feel special.

CORE NYT GAMES:
• Wordle: 1/6=50 (hole-in-one!), 2/6=30, 3/6=22, 4/6=15, 5/6=10, 6/6=6
• Connections: 0 mistakes=30, 1=24, 2=18, 3=12 (gradual penalty)
• Strands: 0 hints=28, 1=25, 2=22, 3=19 (softer penalty)
• Mini: <45s=28, smoother decay to floor of 12

6-GUESS GAMES (Worldle, Tradle, Framed, Moviedle, Heardle, Bandle, Nerdle):
• 1/6=35, 2/6=28, 3/6=22, 4/6=16, 5/6=10, 6/6=6
• Consistent scoring across all X/6 format games

8-GUESS GAMES (Actorle):
• 1/8=35, 2/8=30, 3/8=25, scaling down to 8/8=5

LINKEDIN GAMES (smoother time curves):
• Queens: <30s=30, smoother decay to floor of 10
• Tango: <45s=30, smoother decay to floor of 12  
• Crossclimb: <1min=30, smoother decay to floor of 12
• Zip: <15s=30, smoother decay to floor of 12
• Pinpoint: 1 guess=35, 2=28, 3=22, 4=16, 5=10

GEOGRAPHY GAMES:
• Globle: 1-3 guesses=35, 4-6=28, 7-10=22, 11-15=16, 16-20=12
• Contexto: <10 guesses=35, 10-25=28, 26-50=22, 51-100=16

GS ORIGINALS:
• Quotle: 1/4=50 (hole-in-one!), 2/4=30, 3/4=20, 4/4=12
• Rungs: Perfect=35, 2 attempts=28, 3=21, 4=14
