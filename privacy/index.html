Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 30, 2026 • Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how David Stewart ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Game Shelf ("the Service"), including our website at gameshelf.co and companion games (Quotle, Slate, Rungs, Word Boxing).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo URL.

Game Data: Puzzle game results you record, statistics and streaks, battle participation, friend connections, and your display name.

Transaction Information: Purchase history and payment confirmation from Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers).

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: Features you use, pages you visit, and actions you take.

Device Information: Device type, browser type, IP address, and general location (city/region level).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Track your game results, sync data across devices, connect you with friends, and deliver AI-powered hints
  • Process Transactions: Complete purchases and maintain purchase history
  • Improve the Service: Understand feature usage, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Communicate with You: Respond to support requests and send service-related notifications
  • Ensure Security: Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only in these circumstances:

With Friends

Friends you connect with can see your display name, photo, leaderboard rankings, and activity status. Friends cannot see your full game history or private information.

Service Providers

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Google Firebase Database, authentication, hosting Account data, game data
Stripe Payment processing Purchase transactions
Anthropic AI hints Hint requests (anonymized)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

Data Retention

Active Accounts: We retain your information while your account is active.

Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days.

Transaction Records: We retain purchase records for 7 years for tax and legal compliance.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Export: View your data in the app or request a full export at support@gameshelf.co
  • Correction: Update your display name and profile in app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account anytime via Menu → Account → Delete Account
  • Opt-Out: Sign out to use limited features without an account

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising your rights. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise these rights, contact support@gameshelf.co with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, and port your data. Your data may be transferred to the United States using appropriate safeguards.

To exercise these rights, contact support@gameshelf.co with "GDPR Request" in the subject line.

Children's Privacy

Game Shelf is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS), secure authentication through Google, and Firebase security rules. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

Website: https://gameshelf.co

For privacy-specific requests, include "Privacy" in the subject line.
